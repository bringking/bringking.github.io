About

I am driven by my hunger to understand the world around me. It’s this hunger which makes me a dedicated problem solver, developer or engineer. I love to work on interesting problems, no matter the subject matter. I would like to think this quality makes me a valuable and or interesting person to have around in life or at work.

However, more importantly I’m a husband and father. My family is what ultimately drives me to be a better person and to be successful.
